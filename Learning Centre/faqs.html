Frequently Asked Questions

1. Why is the focus on HTML/CSS/JavaScript only and not other languages?

These 3 languages form the core of frontend web technologies which is high in demand and relevant in today's world.

2. When are the classes carried out?

If the student is enrolled in our full-time program, the lessons are carried out typically on a full day (between 9-5pm) on at least 3 week days. For students enrolled into our part-time program, lessons will be carried out on 2 weekday evenings and on a full day on Saturdays.

3. How does the job placement assistance work?

Towards the end of your course, our talent management team will contact you to link you up with various companies.

4. What are the modes of payment available to pay for the course fees?

We accept the following payment methods: Master/Visa,PayNow,Nets or Cheque (made payable to ABC Training Pte Ltd).

5. Can I pay the course fee in installments if I am not able to pay the entire fee at once?

We will review your case and this is subjected to our approval process.
